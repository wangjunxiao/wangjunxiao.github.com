About Me

I am an Ph.D. candidate at Computer Science Department, Dalian University of Technology from 2016, advised by Prof. Keqiu Li. I received my M.S. from Computer Science Department, Dalian University of Technology in 2017, advised by Prof. Keqiu Li. Before that, I received my B.E. in Software Engineering Department from Dalian Maritime University in 2014.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in computer networking with a special focus on software defined networks, virtualized network function and cloud.
